Chockablock Jumbo Gorilla Enterprises - About Us

Our company's mission is to relocate all of our rescued, endangered gorillas to safe environments, hopefully removing them from the endangered species list. Many of our gorillas were rescued after being wounded from reported poaching incidents, which is the main cause of the species being endangered. We rescue the gorillas and nurse them back to health at our main rehabilitation center, then search for a safe environment for them to be released in, to live a normal life again. We are able to rescue endangered gorillas from any country in the world, which we are very proud of. Our company name is derived from the fact that we have a large amount of gorillas at our main rehabilitation center. Finding new homes for our gorillas has been a challenge, especially with the state of the world at the moment, but we are hopeful, and are looking into developing our own safe zones for the gorillas to stay until they can transition back into the wild.
